RISK MODE GUIDE
🟢
LOW: Needs 0.08%+ move, strong volume, bullish/bearish order book imbalance, AND momentum. WAIT otherwise. Confidence 63%+ minimum. Best for beginners.
🟡
MEDIUM: Fires on 0.03%+ moves. Doesn't require perfect volume. Uses order book + momentum + time-of-day together. Confidence discounted slightly.
🔴
HIGH: Always picks a direction. Uses all signals: order book, 24h trend, rolling momentum, time-of-day bias, ATR slope, volume profile. 50–60% confidence = genuine uncertainty — bet very small.
DATA SOURCES USED
📊
Order Book: Real buy/sell order imbalance from Binance spot market. When buys heavily outweigh sells, price tends to rise in the next 15 minutes.
🕐
Time of Day: BTC has historically documented session biases. NY open (9:30–11 ET) trends bullish, Asia open (8–10 PM ET) is more volatile, and midday is typically quiet/choppy.
📈
Momentum: Rolling price ticks over the last 20 observations. Detects acceleration, not just direction.
⚡
Volatility (ATR): Scales bet size automatically. High volatility = smaller bets. This protects you on wild days.
📉
Volume: Compared against a 35B daily average baseline. Low-volume signals get penalized in confidence scoring.
